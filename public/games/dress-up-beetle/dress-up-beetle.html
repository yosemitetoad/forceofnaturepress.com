Oh no! Stipple the Stag Beetle got ink all over their clothes while making prints in the Force of Nature Press studio and now they're late for a big event! Can you help them pick out a snazzy new outfit?

HOW TO PLAY:
Simply grab clothing from the menu on the left and drag it onto Stipple. Want to change something? Just hover over an item to resize, rotate, duplicate, or delete it.

If you want to move, resize, rotate or duplicate a whole outfit, hold shift while clicking items (or click and drag your mouse over them) to select multiple at once. Use the link button to stick items together!

Have fun, and don't forget to accessorize!
